Our Story

We are a local and family owned Middle Eastern grocery and restaurant tucked away in the heart of our city. For over 35 years, this family-owned business has been providing the community with the most authentic and flavorful Middle Eastern goods. From imported feta cheese and olives to a large variety of teas, coffees, and pickles!

But what truly sets us apart is our commitment to preserving the traditions of our culture. The recipes we use to make our food are passed down from generation to generation, ensuring that every bite is just as delicious as it was decades ago.

The products at our Mediterranean grocery and restaurant are imported from all over the world! From the Middle East to Europe. You can find olives from Greece, coffee from Turkey, feta cheese from Bulgaria, chocolates from Germany, sweets from Dubai, teas from India and so much more. Every item is carefully chosen to ensure the highest quality and most authentic taste.

So next time you're in the market for some Middle Eastern goods and food, stop by our family-owned business and taste the difference..
